      Aljoscha Hehlke

      Senior Recruiter from JustRelate Group, former Infopark Group

      JustRelate helps customers such as B2B companies and public institutions building Digital Experience Platforms (DXP) to digitally bind their customers at economic cost. The headquarter of JustRelate is located in Berlin. We have a second office in the Polish main cultural capital Wroclaw. In addition, many of our colleagues are working in other locations, always where they have their MacBooks, throughout Germany, Poland, and the U.S. We are hiring Java developer of every experience level at the moment so don't miss this opportunity!


      Your Responsibilities


      • You conceive and develop based on our own, object oriented middleware-platform individual CRM-Solutions by adjusting our highly flexible standard-software for the needs of the customer and integration into the existing it system landscape or work directly on the product development
      • Regular communication with our customer
      • Continuing competence building and rising project experience gives you the option to take responsibility as project leader
      • You work as a part of an agile team of experienced colleagues who like to share their knowledge with you


      Your Skills


      • IT related university degree (computer science/engineering) or a comparable training with appropriate background (e.g. as IT specialist)
      • Java knowledge, experience with handling common tools from Java surroundings like Eclipse
      • Database knowledge (Oracle and/or Microsoft SQL)
      • English and German language skills are at minimum fluent level, both written and spoken
      • We care about solid communications skills and continuous self-improvement. Even if you do not have all these skills mentioned above do not hesitate to apply


      Your Benefits


      • Well-founded familiarization with the area of responsibility
      • Lots of space for initiative and work
      • A safety workplace with a high feel good factor
      • Flexible working times and family-friendly working atmosphere
      • Provision of modern work equipment
      • Free choice of work location: home or our fancy offices in Berlin and Wrocław
      • Free supply of fruit and drinks as well as a great espresso machine
      • Regular company social events
      • An open corporate culture


      We are looking forward to get to know you!
